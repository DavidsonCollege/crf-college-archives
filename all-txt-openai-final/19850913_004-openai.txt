IMAC Flickerball

Team rosters are available in IMAC office starting Monday, September 16. The rosters are due with a $10 entry fee (no cash) on Friday, September 20 at 12 p.m.

There will be an important team manager's meeting Wednesday, September 18 at 7 p.m. on the front steps of Chambers. All managers must attend.

A referee clinic and exhibition game will be Wednesday, September 25 — time announced later. Complete schedules will be completed on Thursday, September 26 and will be available in the IMAC office. Play begins Sunday, September 29.

The first IMAC Council meeting will be Monday, September 16 at 7:30 in the Lilly Conference Room of the library. All IMAC representatives are required to attend.
