Venice, January 3

Rain falls hurriedly

Like sand

Melting this winter water-town.
Elegance sticks

Like dirt

And gleams from buildings
At evening's failing.

My friends and I talk

Shut off

in cheerful hotel rooms

Of electric light,
Word games to forget
Dissolving walls.

Clayton F. Houchens

Laura Moses
