At Lexington, Va., the Wildcats defeated the V. M. I. cadets 7 to 5. The cadets held the large end
of the score until the first of the eighth, when Davidson scored four runs on three hits and two hit
batsmen. Neid relieved Crockett in the eighth and retired the Wildcats. Laird pitched a good game for Davidson and in
addition cracked out two hits. Alford, captain of the Wildcats, smashed a double, scoring two men in the eighth.
