Pan-Hellenic Council Presents Midwinters’ Sponsors

Pictured above are the sponsors for the annual Midwinter dances to be held this weekend. In the center is pictured
Miss Diane Coleman, sponsoring the Phi Delta Theta fraternity, with Ed Golden, president of the Pan-Hellenic Council. Starting at the
top and around are: Miss Betty Kerr Hendrix, Sigma Alpha Epsilon fraternity, with John McAlister; Miss Dorothy Mauldin, Sigma Phi
Epsilon fraternity, with Jerold Robinson; Miss Betsy Hatch, Beta Theta Pi fraternity, with Bill Pollard; Miss Joyce Tomlin, Kappa Sigma
fraternity, with Thacker Thrower; Mrs. George McCormack, Phi Gamma Delta fraternity, with George McCormack; Miss Jean Stallings, Phi Kappa Alpha
fraternity, with Jack Lazenby; Miss Helen Potter, Pi Kappa Phi fraternity, with Ernest Hunter; and Miss Katherine Kirkman, Kappa Alpha
fraternity, with Earl Mahone.
