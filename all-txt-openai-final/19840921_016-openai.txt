The Davidsonian proudly announces a fall photography contest for students and faculty.

Entries must be black-and-white prints ranging in size from 3 1/2” x 5” to 8” x 12”. Prints should be mounted, but no mattes, frames, or decorative borders are allowed. Student entries should be mounted on a black board; faculty entries should be on white board.

Entries should be submitted no later than Sunday, October 21, 1984 to: Peggy Pierotti, Photography Editor, The Davidsonian Box 219, Davidson, N.C. 28036. Entries may be mailed or dropped by The Davidsonian offices on the third floor of the Union. Entries should include category, the contestant’s name, address, and phone number on the back of the photo.

The subject matter of photos will be left up to the photographer. Only two entries per contestant will be allowed. All photos must be original, but darkroom work need not have been done by the contestant. Only unpublished and recent (taken within the last six months) photos should be submitted.

Prizes for the contest will be a $50 gift certificate to a Charlotte camera store for first place in each of the two categories, one $25 gift certificate for second place in the student category, and one $10 gift certificate for third place in the student category. Photos will be judged by a panel composed of students, faculty, and administrators.

Questions can be directed to Peggy Pierotti at 892-2000, extension 148 or 892-6277.
