LIST OF COLLEGES AND THEIR DELEGATES

While not all the colleges have sent in their registrations for the conference to be held at Davidson on March 12-14, over 80 have signed up at the present time. This number is expected to be considerably augmented before Friday. The following is a list of the colleges and delegates:

East Carolina Teachers’ Training School (3), Greenville, N. C.: Misses Helen Bahnson, Elizabeth Bass and Sallie Bell Noblin.

Atlantic Christian College, Wilson, N. C.: Misses Hilary T. Bowen, Anna C. Moore, Olive and Lawrence Moye.

Meredith College (5), Raleigh, N. C.: Misses Lillie May Aycock, Beth Carroll, Emma Jackson, Lucile Kelley and Margerite Maddrey.

Louisburg College (2), Louisburg, N. C.: Misses Mary Jenkins and Mary Clay Knight.

Wake Forest College (5), Wake Forest, N. C.: T. C. Allen, S. E. Ayers, R. A. Herring, C. B. Howard and J. R. Nelson.

Trinity College (7), Durham, N. C.: Miss Blance Barringer, Eugene Chesson, J. W. Fitzgerald, Paul Gibbs, Gus Hester, Sam Maxwell and J. D. Pearson.

Elon College (10), Elon College, N. C.: Misses Linnie Daniel and Gladys Foushee, B. W. Everett, J. W. Fix, O. C. Johnson, P. E. Lindley, J. E. Massey, L. P. Rippy, E. M. Smith, M. J. W. White.

Peace Institute (3), Raleigh, N. C.: Misses Dorothy Blount, Eugenia Fairley and Bennie Lee Upchurch.

Greensboro College for Women (8), Greensboro, N. C.: Misses Edith Ayer, Margaret Boylan, Verna Edwards, Elizabeth Ferguson, Naomi Howie, Irene Ormond, Louise Quinn and Mary Wilson.
