THE YEAR IN PICTURES

Rob Kosicki
The youthful football team experienced some growing pains this year, finishing 1-8-1, but next season promises more wins and brighter
days for the Wildcats.

Rob Kosicki
Conference tournament MVP and standout goalie Alex Deegan capped off a storybook career by leading Davidson to the Southern Conference
Tournament Championship.

The women’s soccer team had plenty to cheer about, claiming their second straight conference tourney title.

Rob Kosicki

Men’s hoops dominated the winter sports scene, recording the second most wins in school history (25) and the first undefeated
run in the Southern Conference in 21 years. Said Marshall head coach Billy Donovan, whose team dropped a 49-point blowout
to the Wildcats in January, “Of the teams we've played this year—Kentucky, I put them at a different level from
everybody else, but Davidson is probably the best team that we played.”
