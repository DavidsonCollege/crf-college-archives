ADAM AND REGAL ARE PUSHED FAR BACK. THE DUO ARE QUICKLY LOST IN THE MAZE.

NEO: HO! BROTHERS, BE SOOTUEN! AS INTO THE SEWER, AS THE MELEE OF ROBOTS OF TUNNELS...

DISAPPEARING, RANTING: SURELY AS BLACKNESS AWAITS US...

ADAM: THERE MUST BE A THOUSAND OF THEM!

THOUGH OUR PLANET FALLS TO THE ABYSS, JUDGMENT DAY IS UPON US! UNEDUCATED SUBTERRANEAN MUTANTS, OUR OPPRESSORS, THE SURFACE PEOPLE, SHALL BE CONSUMED, WHILE WE, THE SUFFERING SLAVES, SHALL STAND IN GLORY FOREVER!

SHE WAS A SUCCUBUS, A DEMON IN THE CRAZY! GOING...

HELLO? HI! AWM. SUC... ARE YOU FOSTER, SET BY SATAN IN ORDER TO SEDUCE. WHEN I WAS ABOUT TO STEP OUT YOUR DOOR, "SHE" HAD NO CHOICE BUT TO GO BACK TO HELL.

THEY ARE GONE, PARCEL...

DAVIDSONIAN, Friday 16 October 1981
