McDonald Bereaved
Every member of the student body extends his sympathy to Professor Thane McDonald in the loss of his father on Saturday morning. Professor McDonald received the word of his father’s death on Saturday and soon after caught a plane from Charlotte to Elkhart, Indiana, where the funeral services were held on Sunday. When the Davidsonian went to press, it was not known exactly what day Professor McDonald would return from the North. He will, however, be on the campus the latter part of the week for his regular duties.

Senior Elected Vice-President Of State Christian Group
J. R. “Casey” Jones of Davidson’s rising senior class was elected vice-president of North Carolina’s Student Christian Movement at Chapel Hill Sunday, April 28. This meeting was held at the Lonts Graham Memorial Hall of the University of North Carolina. The Student Christian Movement of North Carolina is a state-wide organization of the YMCA’s and YWCA’s of the North Carolina colleges and universities. Seven delegates from Davidson attended this conference. These men were: Chase Brenizer, Casey Jones, John McCrea, Sherrard Rice, Shaw Smith, Albert Winn, and Charlie Watt. Featured speakers of the meeting were Professor Hornell Hart of Duke University and Ray McCocke, national president of the Student YMCA. Casey Jones is the new president of Davidson’s rising senior class. He served this past year as president of the student body and chairman of the Board of Control. Recently he was tapped into Delta Kappa Omicron and is vice-president and treasurer of the YMCA.
