This is a list of the major committees and groups of Davidson College which we believe are of interest to members of the community. Except for the committees of the Board of Trustees, all the other groups have not been compiled and published together until now.

The chairman or head of each group is printed in boldface.

The letters xo indicate ex-officio or non-voting. An asterisk (*) designates student members of faculty committees.

Information about the time and place of meetings can be obtained from group chairmen.

Further information concerning people on this list can be found in the Student Directory, Campus Directory, and Davidson College Catalogue.

There are a few exceptions, but in general, the power structure of these groups is as follows:

The Trustee committees are subject to the Board of Trustees.

The faculty committees are responsible to the Faculty Assembly with decisions of the Faculty Executive Committee usually binding over the other faculty committees.

The SGA Senate is the executive agency of the Student Government Association, which also has its powers distributed among boards and councils.

Committees of the Senate are subject to the Senate.

Councils are semi-autonomous but subject to the Senate or board.

Clubs are independent organizations usually chartered by the SGA.
