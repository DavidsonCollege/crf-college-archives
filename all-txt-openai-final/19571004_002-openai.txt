Class cuts—Science courses—4 cuts
App. Math—4 cuts
R.O.T.C.—4 cuts
Non-science courses—3 cuts
Natural Frosh-soph.—Chapel—5 cuts
Jrs.-srs.—Chapel—8 cuts
Vespers—6 cuts

In addition, one probationary cut is allowed in each class and each non-academic exercise.

1. All students are asked to keep a written record of the exact dates on which they have been absent from classes, chapel, or vespers.
2. All students are urged to check their official absence records regularly. Beginning October 7, the records will be open for inspection in the old dining hall at the following times:
   Monday—10:20-10:40
   Wednesday—10:20-10:40
   Friday—1:30-2:30
3. Absence cards will be sent after 1 excess absence in chapel or vespers and 2 excess absences in classes, chapel, or vespers. However, cards are sent merely as a reminder and failure to receive a card does not relieve a student of responsibility for an absence.
4. The absence records will be kept as up to date as possible. However, in some cases, absences may not be recorded until four days after the absence occurred.
5. All students are reminded that absences one day before and after one-day holidays and two days before and after holidays of more than one day are recorded as double cuts.
