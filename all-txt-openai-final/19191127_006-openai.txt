Lend Us Your Aid—We Will Return It In the Spring. Work on the Annual has started in earnest and it is the purpose of the staff to send in as much material as possible before Christmas. The more material sent in before the holidays, the sooner the Annual will come out in the spring. While the Annual is gotten out by the Senior class, it is a student body publication and all members of the student body are asked to contribute in any way possible. All who can draw, write poetry, prose, or jokes, take interesting pictures, or offer any suggestion, and all who think they can do any of the above are asked to hand their masterpiece to any member of the Annual staff or to leave them in number 23 Rumble any time between 7:30 a.m. and 11:59 p.m.
