THE DAVIDSONIAN | THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 27, 1990

FAMOUS LAST WORDS
FROM FRIENDS TO FRIENDS...

“Are you OK to drive?”
“What’s a few beers?”

“Did you have too much to drink?”
“I’m perfectly fine.”

“Are you in any shape to drive?”
“I’ve never felt better.”

“I think you've had a few too many.”
“You kiddin’? I can drive — with my eyes closed.”

“You've had too much to drink, let me drive.”
“Nobody drives my car but me.”

“Are you OK to drive?”
