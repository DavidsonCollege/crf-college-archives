W. E. Milton has been selected to edit the athletic department during the coming year. Milton served during the past year as an assistant managing editor of The Davidsonian, having charge of all the assignments. Milton has also been an efficient worker with The Davidsonian Press Bureau. He will have an important position next year, athletics composing about one-fourth of the news in The Davidsonian.

For associate editors, A. Y. Bowie, R. B. Parker, and A. H. Hollingsworth have been appointed. E. P. O’Connor was selected to head the Alumni department. These men have proven their ability in literary lines.
