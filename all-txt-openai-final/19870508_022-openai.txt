Students sign petition in support of Dr. John Engell

We, the undersigned, believe that Dr. John Engell is an outstanding professor and a fine member of the Davidson community.
He is not only a credit to his profession, but a worthy contributor to Davidson’s academic tradition. We hope that
the administration and the Board of Trustees will consider student opinion in this matter.

Friday, May 8, 1987 THE DAVIDSONIAN
