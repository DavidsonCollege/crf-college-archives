To Davidson Students, Faculty, and Staff:

The Trustees, Faculty, Staff, and Students of Davidson College request the honor of your presence at the inauguration of

Robert Fredrick Vagt as the sixteenth President of the College on Friday, the third of October at eleven o'clock Davidson, North Carolina

All of the College Family are invited to attend this historic event in the life of Davidson College and to the luncheon on the front lawn immediately following the ceremony.

Classes are suspended from 10:30 A.M. until 1:20 P.M. The Commons and all houses on Patterson Court will not be serving lunch this day.
