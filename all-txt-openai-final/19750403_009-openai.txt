Sun shines on tennis in many positions

Harvard Beats Wildcats

On Wednesday, April 2, Harvard defeated the Davidson court Cats 6-1. The only Davidson victor was freshman Mike Barnhill, who won his #4 singles match, 6-3, 7-6.

Davidson now has three matches in a row, all on the road. Today the Cats will be in Chapel Hill to face UNC-CH. Then they begin their conference play on Friday, challenging Richmond. On Saturday the team will be hosted by William and Mary.
