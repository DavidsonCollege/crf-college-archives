Campus Events

As part of a local observation of Human Rights Day, the Davidson chapter of Amnesty International hosted an “Amnesty International Coffeehouse” from 7 to 9 p.m. Monday in the 900 Room of the College Union.

Students commemorated Pearl Harbor Day in front of the flagpole last Wednesday by yelling, “Tora! Tora! Tora!” and drinking Kamikazees.
