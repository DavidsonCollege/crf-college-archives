TRACK MEN BEGIN INFORMAL PRACTICE

The following men of last year's track team have returned: G. W. Crouch, Captain Grey, Manager McQueen, Brand and A.
G. Finley. These, with promising material from the Freshman class and several upperclassmen, are showing up well in early practice
which began informally last week.

Besides sending a team to the state track meet at Raleigh, the management has arranged a meet with A. &
E. and is endeavoring to complete arrangements with other teams such as South Carolina and Washington and Lee. Clemson, who
was to appear here this season, has cancelled all engagements. The full schedule will be announced within a few weeks.
