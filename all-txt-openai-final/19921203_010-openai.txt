Duke Senior Co-Captain Chris Yankee:
"The first thing I'd like to do is congratulate Davidson College both on being in the Final Four and hosting it. Bringing the event to North Carolina is something that soccer needed. It's a great thing for the state and for the NCAA's South region. We're very happy that we're in it, and we're trying to take it one game at a time. As far as our semifinal with Virginia is concerned, there won't be any surprises between the two teams. It'll just be a matter of who plays better. It's sort of a backyard conference thing that's been extended to the Final Four."
