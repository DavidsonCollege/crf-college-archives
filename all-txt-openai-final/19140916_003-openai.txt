NEW BOOKS IN LIBRARY.
A few new novels have arrived at the library that the students will do well to take note of. The
library is being well supplied with the very best fiction. The books relating to the different college departments have been
ordered, but have not yet arrived. These will bring the list of supplementary books up to date and enable the
student to get the very best sidelights on his classroom work. The list of new novels, together with their authors,
is as follows: “Truth About Tolna,” Runkle; “The Music Master,” Klein; “John Barleycorn,” London; “Fortunate Youth,” Locke; “Penta,” Tarkington; “Devil’s
Garden,” Maxwell; “Salamander,” Johnson; “You Never Know Your Luck,” Parker. “The New York Evening Post,” one of the best and
most authoritative of the war dailies, has been added to the reading room.
