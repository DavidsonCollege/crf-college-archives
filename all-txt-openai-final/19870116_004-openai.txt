**Friday, January 16, 1987 THE DAVIDSONIAN**

To all these individuals, merchants, and organizations who helped make the 1986 SGA PHONATHON such a success:

THANK YOU!

You raised an all-time record of over $149,000 and had over 1700 people agree to give to Davidson this year.
