Fourth Quarter.

Bunting lost 2 yards; Stewart lost 3; Leech punted 35 yards; Johnston downed in tracks, Spann went over left tackle
for 7 yards; Johnston took 4 over right; Black made 1 yard over right tackle. Spann passed to Douglas for

passed to Douglas for a touchdown. McAlister kicked goal. V. M. I. 14, Davidson 7.

took 3 through center; Leech punted 40 yards to Johnston, who returned 15. Johnston lost 4 yards; Bunting intercepted forward
pass. Leech failed to gain around right end; Bunting took 2 yards off tackle; Leech made it first down; Dickson
went 7 yards. Game over. V. M. I. 14, D. C. 7.
