Tom Fry Leads

Council Meet

At a meeting of the Fellowship
Sunday night, Tom Fry
led the discussion on the “Glory of
the Cross.” The subject for the
next meeting will be “Thy word
have I laid up in my heart, that I
might not sin against thee,” Psalms
119, verse 11.

This will also be the topic under
discussion during the coming
month. While the next
plans to discuss the subject in a
general way, the following meetings
of the month will deal more spe-
cifically with various phases?of the
topic.
