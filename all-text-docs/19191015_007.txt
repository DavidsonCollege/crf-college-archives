The marriage of Miss Mildred John-
ston and Rev. Fred J. Hay. Jr., was sol-

noon, October 14. Rev. Samuel H.
Hay, of Mooresville, a brother of the
groom. and Dr. C. M. Richards, of
Davidson, uncle of the groom. officiat-
ed. While the guests were assembling
Miss Hattie Thompson played the Bri-

ding march from Lohengrin announced
the approach‘ of the bridal party. The
ushers entered first, Will Ramseur with

carrying the ring buried in the heart of
