Forty Montana State College co-
eds will play guinea pig roles this
quarter in tests to determine the
vitamin C adequacy of their habit-
ual diets.

These nutrition tests, conducted
by the head of the home economics
department, are part of a regional
study being made in the northwest-
ern states. Starting simultaneously
more than 150 other coeds at col-
leges in Washington, Oregon, Idaho
and Utah will be undergoing similar
tests,
