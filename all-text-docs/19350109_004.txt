Student Returns From
Visit To South America

Charles Lucas, a member of the senior
class at Davidson, returned yesterday
from a pleasure trip to South America.

Lucas, who is a member of Kappa
Alpha social Fraternity and president
of the International Relations Club, left
this country a few days before Christ-
mas. ‘His destination was unknown,
