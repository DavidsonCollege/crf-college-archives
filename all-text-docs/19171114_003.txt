“LE CERCLE FRANCAIS"

The Senior French class now dignifies
itself with the title of “Le Cercle Fran-
cais.” Having passed the lower stages
involved in the acquisition of French,
they have branched out into original
composition, and into animated conver-
sation in that language. A record of the
proceedings of the “Cercle” will be pub-
lished from time to time in THe Davin-
SONIAN, unfortunately without the ac-
cent marks, with which the printing
presses in this part of the country are not
supplied.

The subject for discussion at present
is “The Land of France,” a topic which
should be generally interesting at this
time. The “Cercle” will welcome the
addition to its membership of qualified
persons, men or women, in the college
or community. The time of meeting is
Tuesday at 8:30 a. m., and Saturday at
10:10 a. m.
