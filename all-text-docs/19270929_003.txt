HOW TO GET A COON COAT

Go to north woods. Prepare
Christmas dinner. Invite all the
raccoons in north woods. Secure
a member of the Miami Chamber
of Commerce to make an after-
dinner speech. Coons will realize
that they are wasting their. time
in the woods. Will decide. to go
to Florida. Will borrow money
and leave skins as security as they
won't need them in Florida any-
way. Coons will go broke in
Florida. Won't be able to redeem
skins. Use some of the skins for
your coon coat. Sell others at big
profit. Make big money and get
coon coat also,
