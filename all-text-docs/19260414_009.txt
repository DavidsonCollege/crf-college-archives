Above is a group of scenes and familiar
faces seen frequently on the campus here.
These pictures were taken by the R. O. T. C.
department and were grouped together for
this special issue. This is the first time
that this cut has ever been used and some of
the things in it will give great amusement to
those who observe it carefully.

In the top picture on the extreme left, the
first platoon of D Company is shown pass-
ing in review. The second platoon is slightly
in the background on the left. To the right
of this is a new scene of an old familiar
spot, Mormitory Row. On the extreme right
some of the prominent men on the campus
are shown as the appeared in military uni-
form their first year here at Davidson. From
left to right they are: Harllee Branch, edi-
tor-in-chief of The Davidsonian for next
year; Jim Pinkney, president of the student
body for next year, and A. H. Hollings-
worth, president of the Y. M. C. A. this
