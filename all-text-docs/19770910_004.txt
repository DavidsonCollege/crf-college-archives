first got here in two words—shell
shock,’’ said freshman Lisa Ryan

came to Davidson.

and her 347 classmates have been
submerged in a flurry of hectic
orientation activities designed to
show them their way around campus
and get them settled into their class
routines.

of Chambers in the dark and the cake
race which got rained out just as most
of the cakes were being given away.
And speeches.
