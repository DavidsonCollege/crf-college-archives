These dances will be held at the Charlotte Armory January 31 and
February Ist. They will consist of a tea dance on Friday afternoon at
five o'clock, followed by the formal dance at ten that evening.
Saturday there will be a tea dance at four-thirty and the informal
dance at eight and lasting until twelve. Each of the fraternities will
have a banquet or similar function at sometime during the week-end.

Music for the dances will be furnished by Will Bradley and his
Orchestra. The sponsors will take part in the figure on Friday night,
and will be joined by the alternates of the various fraternities and their
