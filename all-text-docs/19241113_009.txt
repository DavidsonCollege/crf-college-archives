REPORTERS’ CLUB HONORS

The Davidsonian Staff and Reporters’
Club, last week, elected eleven new men
into the Reporters’ Club. W. E. Milton
read the list of the new men to be re-
ceived into membership. All of the men
mentioned were voted into membership.
P. F. Jones then made a motion that a
feed and an initiation be held for these
men, The motion was carried and C.
McMillan, R. 2B. Parker and W. H./
jranch were named as those composing |
the feed committee. The feed and,
initiation will be held a week or two
later. Following is a list of those voted
into membership: C. L. Bragaw, J. D.
Laws, D. M. Calhoun, C. R. Anderson, C.
C. Murchison, V. S. Broyles, L. L. Deck,
J. P. Gladney, W. T. Davis, G. M. Ed-
dington and F, T. Franklin.

(III

After the election, Milton spoke to the
club concerning the work of the report-
ers. He insisted that the men in writ-
ing their articles would get them in on
time, get accurate information, and have
correct punctuation. He also gave a plan
to let the reporters submit heads for
their articles. The club discussed other
matters concerning the work and growth
of the Davidsonian.
