The mission Sunday schools of the
Davidson Y. M. C. A. are to be re-
opened as soon asthe “flu” situation will
permit. At present the health authori-
ties will not allow any large gatherings.
All plans for the work have already
heen made.

their own superintendents. For these
the Y. M. C. A, appoints a leadet to
help with the work. For others it ap-
points a superintendent. “The schools
with their leaders or superintendents
itre as follows: Cowan's School House

ent; Cortielius—W, B. Sullivan, leader;
