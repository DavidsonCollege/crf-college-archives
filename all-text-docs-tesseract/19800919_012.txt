Union Art Gallery

Interested in owning original animation cel paintings? The Union will
be hosting an exhibit and sale of more than 250 individual

cels—one-of-a-kind, cartoon paintings that have been actually filmed.
The cartoon characters include Bugs Bunny, Daffy Duck, Porky Pig,
Raggedy Ann and Andy, Betty Boop, and Krazy Kat among others.

The exhibit and sale will be Thursday and Friday, September 18 and
19, from 9a.m. until 6p.m. According to a recent article in Family Circle -
magazine, animation art is now the hottest collecting area within the
field of comic art. On hand to answer questions will be a representative of
Gallery Lainzberg, the nation’s best-known specialists in this art form.
