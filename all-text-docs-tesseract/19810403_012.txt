with love

The Tartini Trio, a distin-
guished instrumental ensemble
from Yugoslavia, will be play-
ing in Hodson Hall on Tuesday,
April 7 at 8:15. The program
will feature pieces by Semetana,
Dvorak, and Hayden.

Members of the group are
Dejan Bravnicar, violinist; Ciril
Skerjanec, cellist; and Aci
Bertoncelj, pianist. They are
three of Yugoslavia’s most em-
inent soloist and started the
Trio in 1966 after graduating
from the Academy of Music in
Ljubljana.

Highly acclaimed in their own
country, the ensemble has also
toured in Austria, Italy, France,
Germany, Belguim, Hungary,
and the U.S.

Their performance is part of
the Chamber Music Series, co-
sponsored by the Davidson
College Union and the Music
Department. Admission is free.
