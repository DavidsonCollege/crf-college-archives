This is a list of the major committees and groups of
Davidson College which we believe are of interest to mem-
bers of the community. Except for the committees of the
Board of Trustees, all the other groups have not been com-
piled and published together until now.

The chairman or head of each group is printed in bold-

The letters xo indicate ex-officio or non-voting.
An asterisk (*) designates student members of faculty

Information about the time and place of meetings can be
obtained from group chairmen.

Further information concerning people on this list can |
be found in the Student Directory, Campus Directory and.
Davidson College Catalogue.
