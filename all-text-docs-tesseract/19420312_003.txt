RUSSIAN CLASS

Dr. G. R. Vowles announces
that the voluntary Russian class
is continuing work with thirty
members in the group.

The class meets every Friday
evening at 7:15. Most of the
work is oral. No grades are
given in the class, and no credit
is given for the work.

The new text is especially
adapted for oral work, stated
Dr. Vowles.
