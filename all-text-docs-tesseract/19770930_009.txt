Venice, January 3

Rain falls hurridly

Like sand

Melting this winter water-town.
Elegance sticks

Like dirt

And gleams from buildings
At evenings failing.

My friends and | talk

Shut off

in cheerful hotel rooms

Of electric light,
Wordgames to forget
Dissolving walls.

Clayton F. Houchens

Laura Moses
