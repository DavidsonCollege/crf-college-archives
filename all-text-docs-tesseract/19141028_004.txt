A special train left Davidson at 1:50
Pp. m., carrygin two hundred students to
the game. They were banked solidly
in the bleachers and were led in  con-
tinued cheering and yelling bv “Bill”
Coneland and “Summertime Somer-
ville. as the Red and Black Machine

HOLIDAY SATURDAY.

A general holiday will be given Sat-
urday and a large crowd will attend the
game, at Winston. After much effort
an especially low rate has heen secured—
a round trip fare of $1.55. which, with
the so-cent admission, will put the trip
within the reach of every man on the
Hill. A record attendance is expected
from Winston and surrounding country.

The attendance on the Bible Study
Classes continues to rise, and much in-
terest is being shown in the work. An
interesting record of attendance will be
kept and posted weekly in the Library.

asked to hand the record of attendance
of their respective class to Norman John-
son immediately after the class meets
every Sunday morning.
