Two Spanish Groups
Close 1952-53 Year

With Joint Meeting

On the night of the eighth of
May in the ballroom of the Stu-
dent Union, the final joint meet-
ing of the year was held for the
Spanish Club and Sigma Delta
Pi, national Spanish fraternity. In-
stallation of the incoming officers
occupied the main part of the busi-
ness affairs. The officers for the
approaching year will be: Presi-
dent, Tommy Douglass; 1st V.
President, Dan McCall; 2nd V.
President, Charlie Hooper; and
Secretary, Bill Hardin.

The following program was then
given: Presentation of a skit by
Dan McCall and Bill Hardin; vocal
by Thorny Frick; interpretation of
a Spanish Donald Duck by Bill
Rogers; game for guessing most
Spanish edibles and toastables, won
by Bill Hardin; Spanish love song
and scene enacted by Betty Gehring
and Jinks Jervey; baby drama
adaptation, written and produced
by Nabby Armfield, and starring
Nabby and Betty Gehring; and an
original Spanish-American song
sung by Elgin White.

After the program was ended,
recognition was given Mr. Goldiere,
who is resigning this year as
treasurer, for his incalculable serv-
ice in the past. Mr. Causey, Mr.
Trakas, Mr. Peak, and the faculty
wives and helpers who have as-
sisted tremendously in programs
during the year were then given
thanks for their interest and sup-
port.

As a finale, the ones present
were awarded certificates as ac-
tive members of the Spanish Club.
