LOCAL BOY SCOUTS.

The local troop of Boy Scouts, or-
ganized under the auspices of the Col-
lege Y. M. C. A, is showing good
progress, twenty-one being present at the
last meeting. Fourteen of them have
taken their Tenderfoot examinations and
the others are preparing.” Students of
the college will be welcomed to the
