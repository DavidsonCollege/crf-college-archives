The Davidsonian proudly announces a fall
photography contest for students and faculty.

Entries must be black-and-white prints
ranging in size from 3 1/2” x 5” to 8” x 12”.
Prints should be mounted, but no mattes,
frames, or decorative borders are allowed.
Student entries should be mounted on a black
board; faculty entries should be on white
board.

Entries should be submitted no later than
Sunday, October 21, 1984 to: Peggy Pierotti,
Photography Editor, The Davidsonian Box 219,

ANNOUNCING

Davidson, N.C. 28036. Entries may be mailed
or dropped by The Davidsonian offices on the
third floor of the Union. Entries should in-
clude category, the contestant’s name, ad-
dress, and phone number on the back of the
photo. _

The subject matter of photos will be left up

to the photographer. Only two entries per

contestant will be allowed. All photos must
be original, but darkroom work need not have
been done by the contestant. Only unpub-
lished and recent (taken with in the last six

Prizes for the contest will be a $50 gift cer-
tificate to a Charlotte camera store for first
place in each of the two categories, one $25
gift certificate for second place in the student
category, and one $10 gift certificate for third
place in the student category. Photos will be
judged by a panel composed of students, fac-

ulty, and administrators.
