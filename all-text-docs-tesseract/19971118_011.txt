QUESTIONS ABOUT TICKETS

CAN I GET FREE TICKETS?

Yes. All students with an ID card are entitled to a free ticket to all games. Faculty and staff are allowed to pick up additional tickets
for their spouse/guest. All seats are reserved. If you havea group that wishes to sit together, come as a group or send one member with

several ID cards to pick up a block of tickets. Picking up tickets in advance is advantageous in that you won’t have to stand in the Validyne
line at the arena. |

WHEN CAN I GET TICKETS?

Student/faculty/staff tickets may be picked up as you enter Belk Arena or at the Ticket Office window from 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM on.
the day of the game. The Wake Forest game will be an exception to this rule and tickets may be picked up during working hours from
Monday December | through game day December 3 .

WHERE WILL MY SEATS BE LOCATED?

The first 300 students will receive Red Seat tickets running from the section 8 behind the visitors bench to section 3 (where the President
sat last year). This L-shaped area flanks the Pep Band. After that, students will receive tickets in the sections 104 and 102 about the —
Domino’s Pizza Stand. Faculty/Staff tickets are located in Section 106 on the bench side.

CAN I GET TICKETS To AWAY GaMEs? .
Yes, except for Duke. Duke sells no individual game tickets. However, we generally can provide good tickets to any away games with
advance notice. Tickets to the UNCC Game at Halton Area on the UNCC campus are free to students, faculty and staff as long as our

supply lasts. Generally, the prices for other games range from $7-$10 for Southern Conference games and probably $15-$20 for ACC/
SEC/WAC or PAC-10 games.
